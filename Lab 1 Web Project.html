Tips for Beginners

If you’re just starting out in video games, here are some tips I’d give you:

  1. Start with games that match your interests (adventure, racing, puzzles, etc.).
  2. Don’t be afraid to play on “easy mode” at first it helps you learn mechanics.
  3. Take breaks and don’t play for too long in one sitting.
  4. Play with friends it will makes the experience more fun and less stressful.
  5. Remember: it’s about having fun, not just winning.
