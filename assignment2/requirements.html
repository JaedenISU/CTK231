Retention Requirements

  • Sequence in Audio and Music Production and Interdisciplinary Technologies – Students must maintain a GPA of 2.50.
  • Sequence in Game Design – Students must maintain a GPA of 2.50 overall and 3.0 in the major, plus consecutive Fall–Spring enrollment in the final year.

Standards for Progress

Students must maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.50 and demonstrate competency through an intermediate portfolio review.

Sequences

Accelerated Creative Technologies Sequence

Allows high-achieving students (GPA 3.20+) to take up to 12 graduate credits during undergrad.

Audio and Music Production Sequence

Focus on sound recording, audio production, design, and entrepreneurship practices.

Game Design Sequence

Focus on game design and production with electives in related creative fields.

Interdisciplinary Technologies Sequence

Focus on programming, UI/UX, and motion graphics with electives in sound and gaming.
